Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 2025

üîí TL;DR: Local AI Cat runs completely on your device. We don't collect, store, or transmit any of your personal data or conversations. Your privacy is 100% protected because your data never leaves your device.

1. Our Privacy Philosophy

At Local AI Cat, privacy isn't just a feature - it's the foundation of everything we do. Unlike cloud-based AI services, our app runs entirely on your device. This means:

  • Your conversations never leave your device
  • We cannot see, access, or store your chat history
  • No data is transmitted to our servers or any third parties
  • You have complete control over your information

2. Information We DON'T Collect

Because Local AI Cat operates entirely offline, we don't collect:

  • Chat Content: Your conversations, messages, and prompts
  • Voice Data: Audio recordings or transcriptions
  • Personal Information: Names, emails, phone numbers, or addresses
  • Usage Data: How you use the app, what features you access
  • Device Information: Your device identifiers, IP address, or location
  • Browsing Data: We don't track your activity or behavior

3. Information We May Collect

To provide basic app functionality, we may collect minimal, non-personal information:

3.1 Crash Reports (Optional)

If you opt-in to crash reporting, we may collect anonymous technical information when the app crashes, including:

  • Device type and operating system version
  • App version and crash logs
  • No personal data or chat content is included

3.2 App Store Analytics

Apple's App Store may collect basic, anonymous analytics such as:

  • Number of downloads
  • App usage statistics (provided by Apple, not us)
  • This data is aggregated and cannot identify individual users

3.3 Subscription Information (If Applicable)

If you purchase a premium subscription through RevenueCat:

  • Payment is processed by Apple (we never see your payment details)
  • RevenueCat stores a unique identifier to verify your subscription
  • No personal information or chat data is associated with subscriptions

4. Data Storage

All your data is stored locally on your device:

  • Chat History: Stored in your device's local database
  • AI Models: Downloaded and stored on your device
  • Settings & Preferences: Saved locally
  • Voice Recordings: Processed and stored locally (if you use transcription)

üí° Your Control: You can delete all app data at any time through your device settings or by uninstalling the app. Once deleted, this data cannot be recovered by us or anyone else.

5. Third-Party Services

Local AI Cat may integrate with the following third-party services:

5.1 RevenueCat (Subscription Management)

  • Used for managing in-app subscriptions
  • Stores only a unique identifier for subscription verification
  • Does not have access to your chat data or personal information
  • Privacy Policy: revenuecat.com/privacy

5.2 Apple App Store

  • Handles all payment processing
  • May collect basic analytics (controlled by Apple, not us)
  • Privacy Policy: apple.com/privacy

6. AI Models & Machine Learning

All AI processing happens on your device:

  • AI models are open-source or licensed for local use
  • No cloud APIs or external AI services are used
  • Your prompts and AI responses never leave your device
  • Models are downloaded from HuggingFace (open-source repository)

7. Children's Privacy

Local AI Cat does not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. Because we don't collect any personal data at all, the app can be safely used by all ages. However, parental supervision is recommended for young users.

8. Data Security

Your data security is ensured by:

  • Device-level encryption: Your device's built-in security protects your data
  • No network transmission: Data can't be intercepted because it's never transmitted
  • No servers: No central point of failure or data breach risk
  • You're in control: Only you can access your data

9. International Users

Because Local AI Cat operates entirely offline and doesn't transfer data internationally, there are no cross-border privacy concerns. Your data stays on your device, wherever you are in the world.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any significant changes through:

  • An in-app notification
  • Updating the "Last updated" date at the top of this policy
  • Posting the new policy on our website

11. Your Rights

Because we don't collect your data, you automatically have:

  • Right to Privacy: Your data never leaves your device
  • Right to Delete: Delete the app to remove all data
  • Right to Access: All your data is on your device, accessible to you anytime
  • Right to Portability: Export your data using the app's export features

12. Contact Us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy or our practices, please contact us:

  • Email: privacy@localaicat.com
  • Support Page: localaicat.com/support
