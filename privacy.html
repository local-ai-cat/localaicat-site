Privacy

Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 2025

Local AI Cat is designed around on-device AI. The default experience keeps your chats, models, and voice inputs on your own hardware instead of routing them through our servers.

TL;DR: We do not collect, store, or transmit your conversations. Your data stays on your device unless you explicitly enable an Apple-provided cloud transcription option.

Our Privacy Philosophy

Privacy is not an add-on for Local AI Cat. The app is built so core AI chat and transcription features can run locally, giving you full control over your content.

What We Do Not Collect

  • Chat content or conversations
  • Voice recordings or transcriptions
  • Personal information
  • Usage analytics
  • Device information or location

Data Storage

All app data is stored locally on your device. Deleting the app removes the stored data from that device.

Apple Enhanced Transcription

This optional feature uses Apple servers to improve transcription accuracy and is off by default.

If you enable it, audio processing is governed by Apple's privacy policy. We do not receive or store that audio.

Questions

For privacy questions, email privacy@localaicat.com.
