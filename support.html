Help for Local AI Cat

Answers to the most common questions about offline use, supported devices, privacy, and optional Apple-managed transcription.

Does it really work offline?

Yes. Download models once, then chat and local transcription continue to work without an internet connection.

What devices are supported?

iPhone with iOS 17+, iPad with iPadOS 17+, and Mac with macOS 14+ on Apple Silicon.

Is it free?

Core features including chat, voice transcription, and model management are free.

Is my data private?

Your conversations stay on your device by default. There are no Local AI Cat cloud servers processing your chats.

What about Apple Enhanced Transcription?

That feature is optional and off by default. If enabled, audio is processed by Apple servers under Apple's policies.

Need more help?

Email support@localaicat.com and we will help you out.
