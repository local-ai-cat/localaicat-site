Frequently Asked Questions

What devices does Local AI Cat support?

Local AI Cat works on:

  • iPhone: iOS 17.0 or later (requires iPhone with Apple Silicon - iPhone 12 or newer recommended)
  • iPad: iPadOS 17.0 or later (M1/M2/M3/M4 chips for best performance)
  • Mac: macOS 14.0 or later (requires Apple Silicon - M1/M2/M3/M4)

Does Local AI Cat really work offline?

Yes! 100% offline. All AI processing happens on your device. You'll need internet only once to download AI models, then everything works offline - even on airplane mode. Your conversations never touch the internet.

How much storage do I need?

Storage requirements vary by AI model:

  • Small models: 500MB - 1GB (faster, less capable)
  • Medium models: 2GB - 4GB (balanced performance)
  • Large models: 5GB+ (most capable, requires more space)

You can download multiple models and switch between them.

Is Local AI Cat free?

Yes! Local AI Cat is free to download with core features included:

  • Basic chat with AI models
  • Voice transcription
  • Model management
  • Complete privacy protection

Premium features (like advanced models or custom personalities) are available through an optional subscription.

How does voice transcription work?

Local AI Cat uses Whisper, an advanced speech recognition model, running entirely on your device. Just tap the microphone button, speak, and your words appear as text - all offline. No audio is ever sent to the cloud.

Can I use Local AI Cat for commercial purposes?

Local AI Cat is licensed for personal, non-commercial use. If you're interested in commercial licensing, please contact us at business@localaicat.com.

Why does my device get warm during AI processing?

AI models are computationally intensive, especially on-device processing. Your device's processor works hard to generate responses quickly, which can generate heat. This is normal and safe - your device has built-in thermal management. For best results, use on newer devices with Apple Silicon.

Can I export my chat history?

Yes! You can export your conversations in multiple formats (text, JSON, etc.) directly from the app. Since all data is stored locally on your device, you have complete control over your data.

How do I cancel my subscription?

Subscriptions are managed through the App Store:

  • Open Settings on your device
  • Tap your name at the top
  • Select Subscriptions
  • Find Local AI Cat and tap Cancel Subscription

You'll retain access until the end of your current billing period.

What makes Local AI Cat different from ChatGPT or other AI assistants?

Key differences:

  • Privacy: Your conversations never leave your device (unlike cloud-based AI)
  • Offline: Works anywhere, no internet required
  • No tracking: We don't collect or analyze your data
  • Speed: Instant responses with no network latency
  • Control: Choose and customize AI models yourself

Is my data really private?

Absolutely! Unlike cloud-based AI services:

  • Your chats NEVER leave your device
  • We CAN'T see your conversations (even if we wanted to)
  • No servers, no cloud storage, no data transmission
  • Everything runs locally on your device

Read our Privacy Policy for full details.

The app is slow on my device. What can I do?

Try these optimization tips:

  • Use smaller AI models (they're faster but less capable)
  • Close other apps to free up memory
  • Ensure your device has sufficient storage space
  • Update to the latest iOS/macOS version
  • Restart your device if performance degrades over time

Note: Older devices may experience slower performance. For best results, use devices with Apple Silicon (M1+ or A14+).
