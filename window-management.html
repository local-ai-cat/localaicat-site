Window Management

Local AI Cat includes a Window Management feature that lets you quickly snap windows to different positions using keyboard shortcuts.

Mac App Store Limitation

In the Mac App Store version, Window Management can only control the Local AI Cat window itself. It cannot manage windows from other applications.

This is because Apple's App Store guidelines require apps to be "sandboxed" for security. Sandboxed apps cannot access or control other applications' windows.

What this means for you: You can still use keyboard shortcuts to snap the Local AI Cat window to the left, right, or fullscreen. This is great for positioning the chat window alongside your work.

What You Can Do

  • Snap chat to left half — Position Local AI Cat on the left side of your screen
  • Snap chat to right half — Position Local AI Cat on the right side of your screen
  • Maximize chat window — Expand Local AI Cat to fill your screen
  • Center chat window — Move Local AI Cat to the center of your screen

Why This Restriction Exists

Apple's App Store sandboxing protects your privacy and security by preventing apps from interfering with each other. While this limits some features, it ensures that apps downloaded from the App Store cannot access your other applications without permission.

We believe the privacy and security benefits of the App Store are worth this tradeoff for most users.
